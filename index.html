Frequently Aasked Questions

A hackathon is an event where hackers (thats you!) work in teams to develop a project for a set period of time.

RVGS Hacks is a 12-hour hackathon where hackers will work in teams of 1-4 hackers to develop a project. All high schoolers from participating schools will be allowed to attend for free. The theme will be announced during the event.

You need to sign up for RVGS Hacks before January 15, 2025.

You can bring your own laptop and charger, but we will have some for you to use. If you plan on using one of ours, please mark that option on the registration form.

RVGS Hacks is free for all participants!

Feel free to email us at questions@rvgshacks.com
